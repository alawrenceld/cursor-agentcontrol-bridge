About

Still online, thankfully with better taste

I've been building websites since dial-up made a specific sound. Somewhere between the glitter GIFs and the guestbooks, I picked up design and front-end engineering — and somehow never stopped tinkering with my own homepage. These days I help teams turn messy ideas into interfaces that are simple, accessible, and don't require a MIDI plugin.

"Good design is invisible. Bad design has a rainbow gradient and a spinning globe icon."

When I'm not designing, I'm probably petting Mittens (retired internet cat, four appearances in this site's history) or explaining to younger coworkers what a webring was.
